About Our Chapter

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. Our local chapter brings together students, professionals, and enthusiasts to share knowledge, collaborate on projects, and advance the field of electrical and computer engineering.

Our Mission

We are committed to fostering innovation, providing educational opportunities, and creating a supportive community for engineers and technologists. Through workshops, seminars, competitions, and networking events, we help our members stay current with the latest developments in technology and build valuable professional connections.

What We Do

  • Technical workshops and seminars
  • Industry guest speaker events
  • Project competitions and hackathons
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Networking and social events
  • Community outreach and education

IEEE @ UNC is also a subsidiary of Ram Robotics.

Join Our Community

Membership in our IEEE chapter offers numerous benefits including access to exclusive events, networking opportunities, professional development resources, and the chance to contribute to meaningful projects that make a difference in our community.
